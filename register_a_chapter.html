How to Register a Chapter

Starting a new Paizo Chapter is an exciting way to bring the joy of chamber music to your local community. To register a chapter:

  • Gather a team of motivated musicians and supporters at your school.
  • Ensure you have a designated Chapter Head and at least three founding members.

About Our Tutoring Program

Paizo’s tutoring program is completely free and open to all aspiring musicians. Whether you're just getting started or looking to refine your skills, our program offers personalized 1-on-1 lessons and group sessions to help you grow.

From instrument coaching to music theory workshops, our passionate tutors are here to support every step of your musical journey—at no cost to you.

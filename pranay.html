How to Join

Joining PAIZO is easy! Fill out our online application, attend our welcome session, and start jamming with us. No experience required — just passion for music and community!

Who

PAIZO is a collective of music enthusiasts — composers, performers, educators, and volunteers — united by the love of music and the desire to inspire others. We believe music builds connections and strengthens our community.
